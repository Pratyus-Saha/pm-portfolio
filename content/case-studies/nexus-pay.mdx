## The Origin

Cross-border B2B payments have historically been a black box. Money enters the correspondent banking network, gets passed through three intermediary banks, each taking a fee, and emerges on the other side 72 hours later. We set out to change this.

## Defining the MVP

Instead of trying to replace SWIFT entirely, we focused on a specific corridor: US to Southeast Asia manufacturing. By pre-funding local liquidity pools and settling in real-time on a digital ledger, we could provide instant availability of funds.

## Execution

The hardest part wasn't the ledger—it was the compliance and KYB (Know Your Business) onboarding. We designed an automated onboarding flow that reduced manual compliance checks by 40%, allowing businesses to transact almost immediately.
